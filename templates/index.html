Water Wheels

An inventory management tracking system focused on productivity, transparency, and security.


Productivity

Create, delete, and update items with ease. Items are displayed within a table for quick, accessible viewing.

See which products are in stock at a glance with highlighted low-stock items. Visualize current inventory data with the power of our graphical stock display.

Keep your schedule on track with our built-in calendar and timekeeping systems. Show upcoming events and important dates for your company and log your hours with the press of a button.

Need help? Find a short tutorial under the About tab.

Transparency

Actions taken within the database and admin features are recorded to prevent unauthorized access and keep your company's data safe.

With a registered employee ID, users can input their ID to clock in and clock out. A table displays when users have clocked in and out along with their name and employee ID.

Security

Management of critical data such as user accounts is locked behind specialized admin accounts. These require a special access code to be utilized, restricting these functions.
